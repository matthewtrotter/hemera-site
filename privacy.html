Privacy Policy for Hemera Apps

Effective date: May 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Hemera Engineering Services LLC collects, uses, discloses, and protects information in connection with our websites, iOS apps, and software products, including Hablame Ya! and Intraday Earnings Grid.

Information We Collect

Depending on the product or feature you use, we may collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, account details, support requests, preferences, app content, or messages you send to us.

We may also collect app activity, device and diagnostic information, approximate location derived from your device or network, crash logs, performance data, and other information needed to operate, secure, and improve our apps.

If a product includes purchases or subscriptions, payment transactions are processed by Apple or another payment provider. We do not receive your full payment card number.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide, maintain, personalize, and improve our apps and services.
  • Create and manage user accounts, subscriptions, and customer support.
  • Monitor app performance, diagnose issues, prevent fraud, and improve security.
  • Communicate with you about support requests, product updates, and service notices.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce applicable terms.

Speech, Learning, and Financial App Data

Hablame Ya! may process voice recordings, transcripts, practice responses, or learning progress when those features are used. Intraday Earnings Grid may process preferences, watchlists, app settings, or usage activity related to financial dashboards. These products are intended to provide software features, not legal, tax, investment, or financial advice.

Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us host, operate, analyze, secure, and support our apps, but only as needed for those services. We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or in connection with a merger, financing, acquisition, or sale of assets.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services, including Apple services, analytics, cloud hosting, authentication, crash reporting, payment processing, or customer support tools. Those services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide our apps and services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain security. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information and the feature involved.

Your Choices

You may be able to access, update, export, or delete certain information through the app or your device settings. You can also contact us to request help with privacy-related questions. Some requests may be limited by security, technical, legal, or operational requirements.

You can control many device permissions, such as microphone access, notifications, and cellular data, through iOS Settings.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

International Users

Hemera Engineering Services LLC is based in the United States. If you use our apps from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where we or our service providers operate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and provide additional notice when appropriate.

Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests, contact Hemera Engineering Services LLC at support@hemeraengineering.com.
